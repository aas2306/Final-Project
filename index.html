If you get an answer right or wrong: nothing happens! You can try a question again an unlimited number of times.
When you finish with a question, if you choose to move on to a question of the SAME LEVEL, you'll get one point, and your brain will get a REGULAR connection. If you choose to move on to a question of a HARDER LEVEL, you'll get two points, and your brain will get a SUPER connection. If you decide to SKIP a question, you'll get 0 points, and your brain will get a BLOCKED connection.
Good luck, and have fun!
